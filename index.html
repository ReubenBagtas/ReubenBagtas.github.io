A little about me


For me Coding is Magical. You can literally create anything from scratch. It makes me feel like I have supernatural powers! Everything seems possible when you know how to program. I am fascinated by how it works and by its complexities. The reason why I am so drawn into computer programming has to do with what I am at my core: I am a builder. I have a passion for constructing something new and innovative. In addition, at my core, I am also a problem solver. I love using creativity to solve problems, which is probably why I am ecstatic when I see algorithms or errors on the terminal. Another thing about me is that I put a lot of value into hard work. I pride myself in my work ethic. I am the type of person that does not mind putting in the hours to get the job done. So if you ever need a person that will be able to sit down for 16 hours of the day with two cups of coffee on his desk, 30 tabs on his web browser, and 4 terminals open, I am definitely your guy. Or if you just want to get to know me, feel free to contact me on my email, reubenbagtas@gmail.com. I'm fun to be around and I love meeting people :)
